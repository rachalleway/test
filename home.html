Welcome to Pawnee, Indiana!

The Pawnee Parks and Recreation Department is the focus of the NBC political satire mockumentary sitcom, Parks and Recreation (Parks and Rec.) From the same minds behind The Office, Parks and Rec follows Leslie Knope, the optimistic Deputy Director of the Parks Department, as she leads her staff to work to make their community a better place, despite the pushback from local residents, oafish bereaucrats, and governmental red tape.

The Parks Department & Friends

You've never met government employees like these! The Parks Department of Pawnee, and all of Pawnee for that matter, is made up of one-of-a-kind residents that make the show what it is: a hilarious, chaotic, feel-good mockumentary of small town America.

Around Pawnee

The world-building of Parks and Rec is part of what makes Pawnee so lovable and (almost) realistic. The locations frequented around become synonymous with who our characters are and are the setting for some of the most memorable scenes in the show.

Parks and Rec Past Events

Over the course of the show's run, the Parks Department took on multiple projects across Pawnee in hopes to make the town a better place. Though never easy, and always accompanied with pushback from residents, these memorable town events were cornerstones for the show.
